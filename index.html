How it works

From living room to commentary box.

1

Pick your scene

Any moment from cricket history. The examples are just a starting point, the choice is entirely yours.

2

Set up the mic

Phone camera rolling, Wispr Flow open on your phone or laptop. Both run at once.

3

Call it, with feeling

One minute, one take. Dictate the moment as commentary, and let the emotion show on camera, exactly as you'd call it from the box.

4

Submit both

Your video and your Wispr Flow transcript are submitted together through this site.

5

Bhogle decides

Entries go to the Wispr Flow team, and the final call on every winner is Harsha Bhogle's.

Judging

Harsha Bhogle is the complete decider.

Every entry is reviewed in full, the video and the Wispr Flow transcript together. Marks are built across three things, and the final word on every score is his.

Emotionhow it felt to watch you
Deliverypace, tone, presence on camera
Wispr Flow recordwhat got captured, word for word

All three count. Harsha Bhogle's decision on every entry is final.
